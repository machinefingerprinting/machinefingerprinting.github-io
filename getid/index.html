Machine Fingerprinting


Hello there! We are network security researchers at Lehigh University developing a new way to identify computers. In order to test our ideas, we need your help to collect data. The test will have your computer render a series of images and send the results to our server. We have provided programs to automate the process for your use or you can follow the instructions in the do it yourself section.

We understand that letting strange code run on your computer isn't a great idea, thus all of our code is open source. All the automated programs do is download the browsers (if needed) and open the tests. They do not download anything permanent to your computer and can be simply deleted after the test is complete.
